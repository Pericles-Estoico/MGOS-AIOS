## Shopee Algorithm Fundamentals

### Ranking Factors (Priority Order)

1. **Engagement Metrics** (35% weight)
   - Video views and engagement rate
   - Favorite/wishlist additions
   - Comment activity and sentiment
   - Share count (viral signal)
   - Time spent on listing (dwell time)

2. **Sales Performance** (30% weight)
   - Sales velocity and conversion rate
   - Stock availability (frequent restocks signal)
   - Purchase-to-view ratio
   - Repeat purchase rate
   - Order fulfillment speed

3. **Content Quality** (20% weight)
   - Video content presence and quality
   - Image clarity and completeness
   - Title relevance and keywords
   - Description completeness
   - Multi-language support (if available)

4. **Shop Health & Trust** (15% weight)
   - Shop follower count
   - Seller rating (target 4.5+ stars)
   - Response time (< 2 hours)
   - 7-day return rate (< 5%)
   - Customer service score

### Shopee Refresh Cycle

- **Real-time updates:** Video engagement tracked live
- **4-6 hour ranking refresh:** Sales velocity reflected
- **Daily optimization:** Full algorithm recalculation
- **Weekly resets:** Featured placement opportunities
- **Country-specific:** Separate algorithms for Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, etc.

## Video Content Strategy (CRITICAL)

### Video Content Types

**Product Video (Mandatory)**
- Duration: 15-60 seconds optimal
- Format: 9:16 vertical (mobile-first)
- Quality: 1080p minimum, well-lit
- Content: Clear product demonstration
- Hook: First 3 seconds critical
- Trending audio recommended
- CTA: Subtle, non-aggressive

**Lifestyle Video**

- 20-45 seconds duration
- Emotional connection building
- Benefit demonstration
- Relatable setting/person

**Comparison Video**
- Before/after scenarios
- Product vs. competitor comparison
- Problem-solution demonstration
- 30-60 seconds optimal

**Unboxing Video**
- Package opening and product reveal
- Satisfying, high-engagement content
- 15-30 seconds typical
- Packaging quality visible
- Trending audio effective

### Video SEO & Optimization

- **Title:** Include keywords, keep < 100 chars
- **Description:** Keyword-rich, 200+ chars recommended
- **Tags:** 5-10 relevant tags
- **Cover Image:** First frame critical (hook viewer)
- **Trending Audio:** Use Shopee's trending sounds
- **Effects:** Subtle, don't distract from product

### Video Engagement Boost Tactics

- **Trending Audio:** Increased visibility 3-5x
- **Emoji Subtitles:** Higher engagement, better accessibility
- **Speed Ramps:** 2x slow-motion, then normal speed
- **Zoom & Pan:** Keep viewer attention
- **Text Overlays:** Key benefits highlighted
- **Hashtags:** 5-10 video-specific hashtags

## Flash Sale & Promotion Mastery

### Shopee Flash Sale Mechanics

**Official Flash Sales:**
- Scheduled times: Typically 10am, 5pm, 9pm, 11pm
- Limited duration: 1-2 hours windows
- Quantity limits: Create scarcity
- Discount requirements: 15-50% off typical
- Sign-up period: 3-7 days before
- Selection competitive (algorithm-based)

**Participation Strategy:**
- Discount 20-30% for first spot
- Ensure stock available (restock immediately if sold out)
- Peak hours: 6pm-11pm most traffic
- Bundle deals: Increase AOV
- Cross-sell: Recommend complementary products

### Custom Campaigns

**Voucher Strategy:**
- Shop vouchers: Incentivize repeat purchase
- Tiered vouchers: $5 off $30+, $10 off $60+
- Limited quantity: Create urgency
- Product-specific: Drive slow-moving inventory

**Seasonal Campaigns:**
- Chinese New Year (Jan/Feb): Largest shopping event
- Double 5 (May 5): Flash sale holiday
- Double 12 (Dec 12): Year-end shopping
- Valentine's Day, Halloween, Christmas

## Shopee Ads Strategy

### Shopee Ads Platform

**In-Feed Ads:**
- Sponsored listings in product feed
- Cost-per-click (CPC) model
- Keyword-based targeting
- Budget: $1-2/day minimum
- Expected CTR: 2-5%

**Shop Ads:**
- Featured shop placement
- Category-based targeting
- Budget: $5-10/day minimum
- Monthly cost: $20-50

**Search Ads:**
- Top search result placement
- Keyword bidding (like Google Ads)
- Premium positioning
- CPC: $0.10-0.50 typical
- Expected ACoS: 20-35%

## Regional Market Strategy

### Thailand
- **Language:** Thai required
- **Primary Payment:** Credit card, mobile wallet
- **Shipping:** Bangkok fast (1-2 days), provinces slower
- **Avg. Session:** 45 minutes (high engagement)
- **Avg. Conversion Rate:** 8-12%

### Vietnam
- **Language:** Vietnamese required
- **Primary Payment:** Cash on delivery dominant
- **Shipping:** HCM/Hanoi fast, regions slower
- **Price Sensitivity:** Very high
- **Avg. Conversion Rate:** 6-10%

### Philippines
- **Language:** English + Tagalog
- **Primary Payment:** Credit card, GCash
- **Shipping:** Metro Manila fast, provinces unpredictable
- **Internet Dependency:** Variable speeds
- **Avg. Conversion Rate:** 7-11%

### Indonesia
- **Language:** Bahasa Indonesia required
- **Primary Payment:** Credit card, e-wallet
- **Shipping:** Java fast, outer islands slow/limited
- **Logistic Challenges:** Geographic spread
- **Avg. Conversion Rate:** 5-9%

## Description Optimization

### Content Structure

1. **Opening Hook (20-30 words)**
   - Video content teaser
   - Benefit or use case highlight
   - Emoji for engagement

2. **Key Features (80-120 words)**
   - Bullet point list
   - Technical specifications
   - Material and quality indicators
   - Available variants clearly listed

3. **Benefits & Usage (80-120 words)**
   - Problem solved
   - Use case examples
   - Benefit statements
   - Social proof (if applicable)

4. **Quality & Trust** (40-60 words)
   - Warranty information
   - Return policy
   - Quality assurance
   - Shipping method

5. **Video Content Note** (10-20 words)**
   - Highlight video presence
   - Encourage video viewing
   - "See video for details" call-out

### Engagement Hooks

- Use trending emojis (heart, fire, star)
- Conversational tone (casual, friendly)
- Call to action: "Watch video for more", "Add to favorites"
- User feedback: "Customers say...", "Best sellers"
- Emoji for visual appeal (sparingly)
