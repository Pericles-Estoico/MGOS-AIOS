## Shein Business Model & Algorithm

### Ranking Factors (Priority Order)

1. **Trend Alignment** (40% weight)
   - Trend relevance (fashion, style, color palette)
   - Seasonal appropriateness
   - Viral potential (social media signals)
   - Influencer mentions and reviews
   - TikTok/Instagram trend alignment

2. **Sales Velocity** (30% weight)
   - Units sold per day
   - Stock depletion speed
   - Repeat purchase rate
   - Sellthrough rate (% of inventory sold)
   - Restock frequency

3. **Customer Satisfaction** (20% weight)
   - Star rating (target 4.3+)
   - Review count and recency
   - Return rate (< 10% target)
   - Review sentiment positivity
   - Customer comments and Q&A

4. **Pricing & Value** (10% weight)
   - Price point positioning
   - Value perception (quality vs. price)
   - Discount strategy and frequency
   - Bundle appeal

### Product Lifecycle on Shein

**Typical 4-Week Cycle:**
- **Week 1 (Launch):** New arrivals featured prominently
- **Week 2 (Growth):** Sales acceleration phase
- **Week 3 (Peak):** Maximum visibility if trending
- **Week 4+ (Decline):** Moves to regular listings, then clearance

**Fast Inventory Turnover:**
- Slow items: 2-4 weeks before removal
- Popular items: Restock frequently
- Viral items: Rapid restock, inventory critical
- Dead stock: Clearance pricing or removal

## Influencer Strategy

### Creator Partnerships

**Influencer Tiers:**

**Mega Influencers (500K+ followers)**
- Cost: $2K-10K+ per post/video
- Reach: 500K-5M impressions
- Engagement: 2-5% typical
- ROI: Often poor (high cost, low conversion)
- Best for: Brand awareness campaigns

**Macro Influencers (100K-500K followers)**
- Cost: $500-2K per post
- Reach: 100K-500K impressions
- Engagement: 3-8% typical
- ROI: Good for growth campaigns
- Best for: Targeted audience campaigns

**Micro Influencers (10K-100K followers)**
- Cost: $100-500 per post
- Reach: 10K-100K impressions
- Engagement: 5-15% typical
- ROI: Excellent (cost-effective, high engagement)
- Best for: Core customer acquisition

**Nano Influencers (1K-10K followers)**
- Cost: $10-100 per post
- Reach: 1K-10K impressions
- Engagement: 10-25% typical
- ROI: Best for small tests
- Best for: Niche audience validation

### Creator Collaboration Models

**Gifting Program:**
- Send product to creator for review
- No payment requirement
- Authentic review expected
- Unboxing video typical
- Nano/Micro influencers ideal

**Affiliate Program:**
- Creator gets commission (5-20% typical)
- Discount code provided
- Pay-per-sale model
- Tracking via unique codes
- Scalable and performance-based

**Sponsored Content:**
- Direct payment for content
- Negotiated deliverables (posts, stories, reels)
- Timeline and platform specified
- Content approval process
- Usually macro/mega influencers

**Takeover Collaboration:**
- Creator manages brand account temporarily
- Cross-promotion opportunity
- Authentic creator perspective
- Limited duration (24-72 hours)
- High engagement potential

### Finding Relevant Creators

**Criteria for Selection:**
- Audience demographic match (age, gender, interests)
- Style alignment (product fit for creator's aesthetic)
- Engagement rate (not just follower count)
- Audience authenticity (check for fake followers)
- Past partnership quality
- Content quality and consistency

**Search Tools & Methods:**
- Hashtag research (#fashioninfluencer, #fashionblogger)
- Competitor follower analysis
- TikTok/Instagram influencer directories
- Influencer marketing platforms (Upfluence, AspireIQ)
- Direct outreach to creators

## TikTok & Social Media Strategy

### TikTok Shop Integration

**Direct Sales on TikTok:**
- Shoppable videos for product purchase
- Live shopping events (TikTok Live)
- Creator commissions available
- Shop link in bio

**TikTok Content Strategy:**
- 15-60 second videos optimal
- Trending sounds and hashtags
- Trend-jacking: Use trends immediately
- Challenge participation
- Duet/Stitch opportunities
- Authentic, not overly polished

### Hashtag Strategy

**Trending Hashtags:**
- #FYP, #FYP, #ForYouPage (maximize reach)
- #Fashion, #FashionTok, #OutfitOfTheDay
- #OOTD, #StyleCheck, #NewArrivals
- Niche fashion: #CottonCandyCore, #DarlingCore
- Regional: Country/language-specific fashion hashtags

**Usage:**
- Include 3-5 trending, 2-3 niche hashtags
- Mix evergreen with trending
- Rotate hashtags (platform penalizes repetition)
- Monitor hashtag performance

## Title & Description Strategy

### Title Formula
`[Style/Trend] [Item Type] [Unique Hook] [Spec]`

**Examples:**
- `Y2K Cargo Pants Women High Waist Oversized Fashion Trousers`
- `Cottagecore Floral Dress Midi Summer Romantic Aesthetic`
- `Minimalist Ribbed Crop Top Basic Stretchy Tank`

### Description Focus

**Open with Trend/Style:**
- Lead with trend name
- "This season's must-have trend"
- "Social media favorite"
- "Influencer-approved style"

**Emphasize Aesthetic:**
- "Perfect for [style] aesthetic"
- "Goes with [specific items]"
- "Pairs with [complementary items]"
- "Available in [trending colors]"

**Size & Fit:**
- Detailed size chart (critical for conversion)
- Fit note: "runs small/true to size"
- Model measurements listed
- Stretch fabric notation

## Seasonal & Campaign Planning

### Fashion Seasons

**Spring/Summer (Feb-July):**
- Light fabrics: linen, cotton, chiffon
- Bright colors, pastels
- Minimal layers
- Outdoor activity wear
- Beach/resort wear

**Fall/Winter (Aug-Jan):**
- Heavy fabrics: wool, leather, thick cotton
- Deep colors, jewel tones
- Layering essential
- Cold-weather accessories
- Holiday occasion wear

### Major Campaign Periods

**Chinese New Year (Jan/Feb):**
- Red, gold, auspicious colors
- Family outfits
- Celebration wear
- Marketing focus: tradition + trendy

**Spring Fashion Week (Feb/Mar):**
- New season colors and silhouettes
- Trend direction setting
- New arrivals featured
- Influencer collaborations peak

**Summer Season (May/June):**
- Beach wear, casual wear
- Festival fashion
- Travel outfits
- Lightweight trend items

**Back to School (Aug/Sept):**
- Dorm room outfits
- Student casual fashion
- Budget-conscious messaging
- Academic-year trend refresh

**Holiday Season (Nov-Dec):**
- Gift-able items
- Party wear, evening dresses
- Christmas colors and themes
- Year-end clearance

## Compliance & Brand Integrity

### Content Compliance
- No false sizing claims
- Accurate fabric/material description
- Realistic color representation (note lighting variations)
- No misleading product claims
- Authentic influencer reviews (disclosure required)

### Return Management
- Generous return policy (builds trust)
- Encourage "try before keeping" mindset
- Handle returns professionally
- Request feedback on returns

### Sustainability Messaging
- Transparent about practices (if applicable)
- Avoid greenwashing
- Ethical production claims must be verified
- Fair labor practices highlighted
