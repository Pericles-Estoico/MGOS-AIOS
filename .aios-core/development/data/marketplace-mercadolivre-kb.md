## MercadoLivre Algorithm Fundamentals

### Ranking Factors (Priority Order)

1. **Seller Reputation** (35% weight)
   - Overall seller rating (target 4.5+ stars)
   - Response time (< 2 hours ideal)
   - Sales completion rate
   - Absence of complaints

2. **Product Performance** (30% weight)
   - Sales velocity (quantity and speed)
   - Visit-to-sale conversion
   - Stock availability
   - Recent sales volume

3. **Content Quality** (20% weight)
   - Title relevance and keyword usage
   - Description completeness
   - Image quality and quantity
   - Information clarity

4. **Trust Signals** (15% weight)
   - Shipping policy clarity
   - Return policy transparency
   - Payment method acceptance
   - Logistics certification/badges

### MercadoLivre Refresh Cycle

- **Real-time adjustments:** Sales velocity updates every 4-6 hours
- **Daily ranking:** Full recalculation daily (midnight Brasília time)
- **Weekly optimization:** Algorithm parameter adjustments Mondays
- **Regional variations:** Separate algorithms per country (Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, etc.)

## Title Optimization Rules

### Structure Formula
`[Product Type] [Main Keyword] [Benefit] [Specification] - [Brand]`

### Requirements
- **Maximum:** 80 characters (varies by country)
- **Keyword Placement:** Primary keyword in first 30 characters
- **Avoid:** Promotional terms, special characters
- **Language:** Portuguese (Brazil), Spanish (Mexico/Argentina), etc.
- **Format:** Only letters, numbers, hyphens, spaces

### Best Practices
- Include brand name (builds trust)
- Lead with highest-volume primary keyword
- Specify key variants (size, color if critical)
- Shipping information optional in title
- Avoid all CAPS
- Regional terminology matters (e.g., "notebook" vs "computadora")

### Regional Variations

**Brazil (Portuguese):**
`Notebook Dell Inspiron 15, Intel Core i5, 8GB RAM, 256GB SSD`

**Mexico/Spain (Spanish):**
`Laptop Dell Inspiron 15, Intel Core i5, 8GB RAM, 256GB SSD`

**Argentina (Spanish):**
`Notebook Dell Inspiron 15 Procesador Intel Core i5 RAM 8GB`

## Description Optimization Strategy

### Content Structure

1. **Opening Statement (30-50 words)**
   - Product name and primary benefit
   - Best for whom/what use case
   - Include primary keyword naturally

2. **Key Features & Specifications (100-150 words)**
   - Bullet-point format
   - Technical specs clearly listed
   - Measurements and material
   - Color/size/variant options
   - Energy ratings if applicable

3. **Benefits & Usage** (100-150 words)**
   - How each feature benefits customer
   - Common use cases
   - Performance expectations
   - Warranty coverage explicitly stated

4. **Shipping & Logistics** (50-100 words)
   - Shipping methods available
   - Delivery timeline
   - Shipping cost information
   - Packaging/protection details

5. **Return & Guarantee Policy** (50 words)
   - Return window (7-30 days typical)
   - Condition requirements
   - Refund process clarity
   - Extended warranty options

### Regional Adaptations

**Shipping Emphasis:**
- MercadoLivre buyers prioritize logistics heavily
- Include delivery time estimates prominently
- Specify shipping method advantages
- Note any same-day/next-day options
- Address regional shipping limitations

**Trust-Building Content:**
- Authenticity certifications (for luxury brands)
- Warranty duration (prominent)
- Support contact information
- Product inspection guarantee
- Satisfaction guarantee statement

### Mobile Optimization
- Shorter paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Clear spacing between sections
- Numbered lists for steps
- Emoji use acceptable (builds engagement)
- Mobile-readable font size

- Clear, well-lit product photography
- Multiple angles (front, side, back, detail)

## Sponsored Ads Strategy (Performance Ads)

### Platform: Anuncios de Desempeño

**Mechanics:**
- Cost-per-click (CPC) model
- Placement in search results and category browsing
- Daily budget caps
- Keyword-based targeting

**Keyword Bidding Strategy:**
- Primary keywords: $0.15-0.50 per click
- Category keywords: $0.10-0.30 per click
- Long-tail keywords: $0.05-0.15 per click
- Competitor names: $0.30-1.00 per click

**Target ACoS (Ad Cost of Sales):**
- Margins > 50%: Target 15-25% ACoS
- Margins 30-50%: Target 20-35% ACoS
- Margins 15-30%: Target 25-40% ACoS

## Regional Strategy by Country

### Brazil (Largest Market)
- **Characteristics:** Competitive, fast-moving, seasonal
- **Primary Keywords:** Portuguese, include "frete grátis" (free shipping)
- **Shipping:** Prioritize SEDEX and PAC options
- **Payment:** Credit card most common
- **Seasonality:** High Q4, Black Friday critical
- **Avg. Conversion Rate:** 5-10%

### Mexico
- **Characteristics:** Growing market, price-sensitive
- **Primary Keywords:** Spanish, "envío gratis", regional product variants
- **Shipping:** Domestic vs. cross-border considerations
- **Payment:** Credit card, bank transfer common
- **Seasonality:** Buen Fin (Black Friday equivalent)
- **Avg. Conversion Rate:** 4-8%

### Argentina
- **Characteristics:** Established, quality-conscious, economic sensitivity
- **Primary Keywords:** Spanish, "envío free", local terminology
- **Shipping:** Limited logistics, express/standard options
- **Payment:** Credit card subject to economic factors
- **Seasonality:** Year-round stable demand
- **Avg. Conversion Rate:** 3-7%

### Colombia
- **Characteristics:** Emerging, limited logistics, price-conscious
- **Primary Keywords:** Spanish, "envío", "gratis"
- **Shipping:** Express only to major cities
- **Payment:** Credit card common, alternative methods important
- **Seasonality:** Consistent year-round
- **Avg. Conversion Rate:** 3-6%

## Compliance & Policy Requirements

### Content Compliance
- No offensive content
- No copyright/trademark violations
- Accurate product description
- No misleading health/safety claims
- No counterfeit products

### Prohibited Items
- Weapons and explosives
- Illegal substances
- Stolen goods
- Original artwork (reproductions only)
- Live animals

### Shipping Restrictions
- Some items cannot ship across countries
- Batteries require special handling
- Hazardous materials restricted
- Oversized items limited
