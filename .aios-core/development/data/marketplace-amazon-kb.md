## A9 Algorithm Fundamentals

### Ranking Factors (Priority Order)

1. **Relevance** (40% weight)
   - Title keyword match
   - Product category accuracy
   - Description keyword integration
   - Customer search query matching

2. **Sales Velocity** (30% weight)
   - Recent sales volume and speed
   - Week-over-week trend
   - Seasonal adjustment factors
   - Stock depletion speed

3. **Conversion Rate** (15% weight)
   - Percentage of viewers who purchase
   - Time to purchase (faster better)
   - Cart abandonment rate
   - Return rate (lower better)

4. **Reviews & Rating** (10% weight)
   - Number of reviews (more better)
   - Average rating (4.2+ target)
   - Review recency (recent better)
   - Review relevance to search term
   - Review sentiment positivity

5. **Other Factors** (5% weight)
   - FBA vs. FBM (FBA advantage)
   - Prime eligibility
   - Seller rating and metrics
   - Page velocity and load time

### A9 Refresh Cycle

- **Real-time adjustments** - Sales velocity updated every 2 hours
- **Daily rankings** - Full ranking recalculation daily (midnight PST)
- **Weekly optimization** - Algorithm parameter adjustments Tuesdays
- **Seasonal reset** - Category-wide reset quarterly

## Title Optimization Rules

### Structure Formula
`[Brand] [Main Keyword] [Benefit/Feature] [Variant/Size] [Material/Color]`

### Requirements
- **Maximum:** 255 characters (including spaces)
- **Minimum:** 40 characters recommended
- **Keyword Placement:** Primary keyword in first 3-5 words
- **Character Types:** Letters, numbers, [brackets], (parentheses), hyphens, commas only
- **Avoid:** Special symbols !@#$%^&*, multiple consecutive spaces, all CAPS

### Best Practices
- Front-load your highest-volume primary keyword
- Include key variants that customers search for
- Avoid keyword stuffing (unnatural phrasing)
- Include numeric specifications (watts, ounces, etc.) if relevant
- Brand name in title (benefits conversion)
- Use only necessary variant info (color/size placement matters)

### Prohibited Words
- Superlatives without verification: "best", "premium" (use with caution)
- Health claims (unless medical device): "cures", "prevents", "treats"
- Call-to-action in title: "buy", "save", "order"
- Promotional terms: "free", "clearance", "limited"
- Competitor names (unless explicitly compatible)

### Examples by Category

**Electronics:**
`Sony WH-XB900N Wireless Headphones, Noise Cancelling Bluetooth Earbuds, 30-Hour Battery`

**Fashion:**
`COOFANDY Women's Sleeveless Summer Dress, Casual A-Line Short Mini Dresses`

**Home & Kitchen:**
`Instant Pot Duo Plus 6-Quart Electric Pressure Cooker, 9-in-1 Programmable`

## Description Optimization Strategy

### Content Structure

1. **Opening Hook (30 words max)**
   - Address main benefit or problem solved
   - Grab attention immediately
   - Include primary keyword naturally

2. **Feature-Benefit Bridge (50-100 words)**
   - Translate each feature to customer benefit
   - Use "helps you" or "allows you to" phrasing
   - Make value proposition crystal clear

3. **Detailed Description (150-200 words)**
   - Organized bullet points or short paragraphs
   - Key specifications listed clearly
   - Use formatting for scannability
   - Address common objections proactively

4. **Trust & Safety (50 words)**
   - Warranty coverage details
   - Return policy clarity
   - Customer support accessibility
   - Certifications or safety standards

5. **Call-to-Action (20 words)**
   - Clear next step messaging
   - Purchase confidence statement
   - "Add to Cart" mentality

### Keyword Integration

- **Density target:** 1-2% (natural integration)
- **Primary keywords:** Include 2-3 times throughout
- **Secondary keywords:** Include 1-2 times naturally
- **Long-tail variations:** Include plural and related terms
- **Never keyword stuff** - Readability first

### Mobile Optimization
- Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)
- Bullet points for lists
- Numeric/measurement data clearly formatted
- Font size considerations for smaller screens
- Line breaks for visual clarity

## Sponsored Ads Strategy

### Sponsored Products (Pay-Per-Click)

**Mechanics:**
- Cost-per-click (CPC) bidding model
- Placement in search results and product pages
- Daily budget caps available
- Automatic or manual targeting

**Keyword Targeting Strategy:**
- **Broad:** Matches variations and synonyms
- **Phrase:** Matches exact phrase and variations
- **Exact:** Matches only exact keyword

**Target ACoS (Ad Cost of Sales):**
- Margins > 40%: Target 15-25% ACoS
- Margins 25-40%: Target 20-35% ACoS
- Margins < 25%: Target 25-40% ACoS

### Sponsored Brands

**Features:**
- Display brand logo, up to 3 products
- Headline + benefit messaging
- Brand store landing page link

**Best For:**
- Category dominance
- New category entry
- Brand awareness campaigns
- Multiple SKU promotion

### Sponsored Display

**Features:**
- Audience targeting (interest-based, pixel-based, product-based)
- Remarketing to past visitors
- Dynamic ads based on browsing history

**Best For:**
- Remarketing past browsers
- Category buyers (lookalike)
- Competitor product pages

## Compliance & Policy Requirements

### Prohibited Content
- Cannot claim to cure, treat, or prevent disease
- No unapproved health or safety claims
- No competitor disparagement
- No call-to-action in product content
- No external website links
- No pricing/promotion beyond price field

### Required Disclosures
- Warnings for hazardous materials
- Allergen information (food, supplements)
- Usage instructions for power tools
- Safety information prominently displayed
- Authenticity for luxury brands

### Return & Refund Policy
- Must honor Amazon's A-to-Z Guarantee
- Default 30-day return window (can extend to 90)
- Restocking fees prohibited (FBA)
- Seller-fulfillment rules apply for FBM

### Seller Metrics Standards
- Order defect rate: < 1% (critical)
- Cancellation rate: < 2.5%
- Valid tracking: 95%+ compliance
- Response time: < 24 hours

## Review Strategy & Management

### Review Generation

**Incentivized Review Rules (Post-Delivery Follow-Up):**
- ✓ Can request reviews via email (timing: 3-7 days post-delivery)
- ✗ Cannot offer incentive for positive review
- ✗ Cannot require account to leave review
- ✓ Can offer discount on future purchase (not conditional on review)

**Organic Review Tactics:**
- Quality product = natural reviews
- Follow-up email 3-7 days post-delivery
- Easy contact method for issues (prevents negative reviews)
- Amazon Vine program (select products)

### Review Monitoring

**Daily Monitoring:**
- Watch for new negative reviews
- Respond within 24 hours to 1-2 star reviews
- Address specific complaints
- Offer resolution (replacement, refund)

**Negative Review Response Strategy:**
- Don't argue or be defensive
- Acknowledge concern
- Offer specific resolution
- Request follow-up review after fix
- Keep tone professional and helpful

### Return Rate Management

**Target:** < 3% return rate (critical threshold)

**Reduction Strategies:**
- Detailed size/fit information
- Multiple photos from different angles
- Accurate description (expectation matching)
- Quality assurance (minimize defects)
- Clear usage instructions
- Fast/free returns (builds trust)

## FBA vs. FBM Comparison

### FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon)

**Advantages:**
- Algorithmic ranking boost (20% advantage)
- Prime eligibility (critical for conversion)
- Amazon handles returns
- Seller doesn't handle shipping
- 24/7 customer service included
- Lower cancellation rates

**Disadvantages:**
- Fulfillment fees (approx 30-45% cost)
- Inventory storage fees
- Less control over inventory
- Commingling risk (product mixing)

**When to Use:** Competitive categories, high volume, profit margin > 30%

### FBM (Seller Fulfilled)

**Advantages:**
- Lower fees (just referral fee 8-15%)
- Full inventory control
- No storage limits
- Brand reputation control
- Higher profit margins

**Disadvantages:**
- No Prime badge (conversion hit ~40%)
- Manual shipping handling
- Manual return processing
- Lower ranking (algorithm penalty)
- Higher defect risk

**When to Use:** Bulk items, low volume, high margin, logistics advantage
