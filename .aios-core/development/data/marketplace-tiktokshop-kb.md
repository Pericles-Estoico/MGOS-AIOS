4. **Shop Health & Community** (10% weight)
   - Shop follower growth
   - Customer reviews (rating and count)

## Creator-First Strategy

### Creator Partnerships Over Paid Ads

**The TikTok Shop Paradigm Shift:**
- Organic creator content outperforms paid ads 3-5x
- Affiliate commissions more efficient than ad spend
- Authentic creator reviews build trust
- Viral potential exponentially higher with creators
- Cost-per-acquisition dramatically lower

### Creator Engagement Funnel

**Tier 1: Awareness Creators**
- Size: 100K-1M+ followers
- Engagement: 3-8%
- Partnership: Gifting program
- Expected reach: 100K-500K impressions
- Cost: Product value ($20-200)
- ROI: Awareness and traffic

**Tier 2: Growth Creators**
- Size: 10K-100K followers
- Engagement: 5-15%
- Partnership: Affiliate (5-15% commission)
- Expected reach: 10K-100K impressions
- Cost: Commission only (performance-based)
- ROI: Conversions and repeat traffic

**Tier 3: Community Creators**
- Size: 1K-10K followers
- Engagement: 10-25%
- Partnership: Affiliate + exclusive codes
- Expected reach: 1K-10K impressions
- Cost: Commission only
- ROI: Highly engaged audience

### Creator Recruitment Strategy

**Finding Creators:**
- Use TikTok creator search (filterable by niche)
- Monitor competitor shops for active creators
- Search hashtags #productreview #unboxing
- Use influencer platforms (HypeAuditor, Upfluence)
- Direct outreach to creators in your niche

**Outreach Message Template:**
```
"Hi [Creator Name]! We love your [specific content type] content.
We think our [product] would be perfect for your audience.
Would you be interested in a [partnership type] collaboration?
We offer [terms: commission rate/product value].
Check us out: [shop link]"
```

**Selection Criteria:**
- Audience demographic match (not follower count)
- Content quality and consistency
- Engagement rate (not fake followers)
- Brand safety (content alignment)
- Audience authenticity
- Past partnership success

## Live Commerce Strategy

### TikTok Live for Sales

**Live Commerce Mechanics:**
- Schedule 3-7 days in advance
- Host (you) + Creator (endorser) model
- Real-time product demonstrations
- Chat interaction with viewers
- Shopping cart visible during stream
- Performance tracked by TikTok analytics

**Live Session Best Practices:**

**Pre-Stream:**
- Announce 3-7 days before
- Provide creator with product info
- Test audio/video quality
- Prepare talking points
- Have inventory ready
- Set up discount codes for live viewers

**During Stream:**
- Start with energy and excitement
- Creator natural testimonial (not scripted)
- Product demonstrations (usage, features)
- Q&A from chat (respond in real-time)
- Limited-time deals (create urgency)
- Multiple products shown
- Clear CTA ("Add to cart now")
- Thank viewers and creators

**Post-Stream:**
- Share highlights as videos
- Repurpose best moments as TikToks
- Thank creator publicly
- Respond to comments
- Follow up with viewers who inquired

**Timing Optimization:**
- **Peak Hours:** 6pm-11pm typical
- **Day Selection:** Weekdays 7pm-9pm strong
- **Weekend:** 3pm-8pm good
- **Frequency:** 2-3x per week sustainable
- **Duration:** 30-90 minutes optimal

### Live Commerce Revenue Model

**Commission Structure for Creator:**
- Standard: 5-15% commission on sales
- Tiered: 5% first $1K, 10% after
- Hybrid: Flat fee + commission
- Exclusive: Higher commission for exclusivity

**Expected Metrics:**
- 100 viewers → 5-15 purchases typical
- $10-50 average order value
- 10-20% of stream revenue to creator
- 30-day payment processing

## Video Content Strategy (CRITICAL)

### Video as Your Primary Sales Tool

**Video is not supplemental on TikTok Shop — it's essential.**

### Content Types for Sales

**Product Demo Videos (15-60 sec)**
- Clear demonstration of usage
- Problem-solution framing
- Multiple angles of product
- "Satisfying" element (if applicable)
- Trending audio/sound
- Hook in first 3 seconds

**Unboxing Videos (20-45 sec)**
- ASMR-style unboxing popular
- Product reveal surprise moment
- Packaging quality highlight
- Product quality visible
- Trending music/sounds
- Satisfying reveal pacing

**Before/After Videos (15-30 sec)**
- Transformation focus
- Problem clearly established
- Product benefit obvious
- Emotional impact
- "Wow factor" moment

**Lifestyle/Usage Videos (20-45 sec)**
- Product in real-world setting
- Relatable person using product
- Natural, unscripted feel
- Problem-solving visible
- Emotional connection
- Social proof ("people are using this")

**Educational Content (30-60 sec)**
- How-to guide for product usage
- Tips and hacks for product
- Troubleshooting guidance
- Educational value builds trust
- Authority positioning

**Trending Content (15-30 sec)**
- Participate in trending challenges
- Use trending audio/sounds
- Adapt trends to your product
- Quick, entertaining format
- High engagement potential

### Video Optimization Elements

**Hook (First 3 Seconds):**
- Surprising statement or visual
- Problem statement
- "Wait for the end"
- Question hook
- Transformation preview

**Audio:**
- Use trending sounds (curated list on TikTok)
- Ambient sound for authenticity
- Music aligned with content mood
- Voiceover if needed (clear, not robotic)
- Captions for accessibility

**Format:**
- Vertical 9:16 ratio (mobile native)
- 1080p resolution minimum
- Bright, well-lit footage
- Smooth transitions
- Pacing: Quick cuts maintain engagement

**Text & Graphics:**
- On-screen text for key messages
- Emoji overlays for engagement
- Benefit callouts
- "Shop now" CTA
- Limited text (visual-first medium)

**Call-to-Action:**
- Subtle product tap/link prompt
- "Link in bio" (shop URL)
- "Available now" messaging
- Urgency language ("while supplies last")
- Non-aggressive (let product speak)

## Short-Form Video Mechanics

### Watch Time Optimization

**Algorithm watches:**
- How long people watch (completion rate)
- When people drop off (watch time distribution)
- If people rewatch (repeat engagement)
- If people share (viral signal)
- If they interact (likes, comments)

**Target Metrics:**
- Completion rate: 50%+ (aim for 75%+)
- Share rate: 5%+ (strong signal)
- Comment rate: 10%+ (engagement signal)
- Average watch time: 7-15 seconds (for 30-sec video)

### Viral Mechanics

**Content That Goes Viral:**
- **Satisfying** - ASMR, transformation, before/after
- **Surprising** - Unexpected twist, shocking result
- **Emotional** - Makes people feel something
- **Shareable** - People want to send to friends
- **Relatable** - "This is me" sentiment
- **Trending** - Uses trending audio/hashtag
- **Short & Sweet** - Respects viewer attention

**Amplification Factors:**
- Creator mentions (massive boost)
- Paid promotion (initial push)
- Posting frequency (consistent presence)
- Engagement rate (interactions boost reach)
- Virality compounds (each share multiplies reach)

## TikTok Shop Ads Integration

### TikTok Ads Manager

**Advertising Options:**
- **In-Feed Ads:** Native-looking ads in home feed
- **TopView:** Premium placement (expensive)
- **Branded Hashtag Challenge:** Community participation
- **Shop Ads:** Direct shop promotion

**Campaign Setup:**
- Budget: Start with $5-10/day
- Audience: Detailed targeting available
- Creative: Usually best-performing organic video
- Landing: Direct to product or shop
- Goal: Conversions, traffic, or awareness

**Budget Allocation:**
- 70% organic creators
- 20% affiliate/incentives
- 10% paid ads
- Note: Many successful shops don't use paid ads

## Shop Building & Community

### Shop Credibility
- **Shop Name:** Brand name preferred
- **Shop Logo:** Clear, professional
- **Shop Banner:** Brand aesthetic
- **Bio:** Clear value proposition
- **Links:** Shop policies, contact, socials

### Review & Rating Management
- Target: 4.5+ star rating
- Review management: Respond to all reviews
- Negative reviews: Address professionally
- Encourage positive reviews: Thank buyers, ask for feedback
- Review rate: Aim for 10%+ of buyers

### Community Building
- **Respond to Comments:** < 4 hours
- **Community Tab:** Post behind-the-scenes, tips, polls
- **Go Live Regularly:** 2-3x weekly
- **Engage Followers:** Reply to DMs, answer questions
- **Build Relationships:** Remember loyal customers

## Compliance & Guidelines

### Content Policy
- No misleading claims
- No health/medical claims (unless medical device)
- No copyrighted content (music in videos)
- No spam or repetitive content
- Age-appropriate content
- No private information

### Shipping & Returns
- Clear shipping timelines
- Realistic delivery expectations
- Easy return process
- Refund policy transparent
- Customer service responsive
